Hard Wired:

 Skill Tier 15% Cooldown Reduction 15% Skill Damage / 15% Skill Repair

Feedback Loop

Whenever you use or cancel a skill, your other skill is automatically refreshed and increases total skill damage and repair by 10% for 20 seconds. This can occur at most once per 20 seconds.

Short Circuit

Decreases Feedback Loop cooldown from 20 seconds to 10 seconds.

Positive Reinforcement

Increases Feedback Loop skill damage and repair bonus from 10% to 25%.

Ongoing Directive:

 Weapon Damage 15% Status Effects 20% Reload Speed

Rules of Engagement

Killing a status affected enemy drops Hollow-Point Ammo. Hollow-Point Ammo amplifies weapon damage by 20% and applies Bleed on hit.

Emergency Requisition

Reloading your weapon automatically collects all combat resources (armor kits, ammo, grenades) within 40 meters.

Parabellum Rounds

Increases Hollow-Point Ammo damage to 35%.

True Patriot:

 Armor 30% Ammo Capacity 30% Magazine Size

Red, White, and Blue

Enemies you shoot receive a stacking debuff of Red / White / Blue, cycling every 2 seconds. Red: Amplifies the enemy's damage taken by 8%. White: Hitting the enemy restores you and your allies' armor by 5% (1s cooldown). Blue: Decreases the enemy's damage dealt by 8%.

Full Flag

Enemies that die while under the effect of all 3 debuffs create a 5 meter explosion, dealing damage equal to their total health and armor. (Explosion strength reduced on Named enemy deaths)

Patriotic Boost

Increases Red / White / Blue debuff strength. Red: 8% to 12%. White: 5% to 8%. Blue: 8% to 12%.

Waving the Flag

Increases Red / White / Blue rotation speed to 1.5 seconds.

Aces and Eights:

 ? 15% Marksman Rifle Damage 20% Headshot Damage

Dead Man's Hand

Flip a card when landing shots with a Marksman Rifle. After 5 cards are flipped, the damage of your next shot is amplified by 30%. More shots are enhanced, the better the hand revealed. Four of a Kind: 4 shots. Full House: 3 shots. Aces and Eights: 2 shots.

Poker Face

Flip an additional Dead Man's Hand card on 1 shot.

No Limit

Increases Dead Man's Hand damage bonus from 30% to 50%.

Negotiator's Dilemma:

 ? 15% Critical Hit Chance 20% Critical Hit Damage

Crowd Control

Critical hits mark enemies for 20 seconds, up to 3 marks total. When you critically hit a marked enemy, all other marked enemies take 60% of the damage dealt. Whenever a marked enemy dies, gain 2% critical hit damage, stacking up to 20 times, until combat ends.

Critical Measures

Increases Crowd Control damage to additional marked enemies from 60% to 100%.

Target Rich Environment

Increases Crowd Control mark count from 3 to 5.

Crossbow Expert

Specialized Destruction

Striker's Battlegear:

Striker's Gamble

Weapon hits amplify total weapon damage by 0.5%, stacking up to 50 times. 1 stack is lost each second. 3 stacks are lost every time you miss.

Press the Advantage
